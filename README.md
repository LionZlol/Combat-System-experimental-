# Combat-System-experimental-

Soo i basically was messing around with js and got really bored and decided to make a full game with js so here's a cool thing hope you like it also these are the controls/tutorial:


fight: does damage directly to an enemy

item: temporary buffs/perks that go away once that item is used up

magic: attacks that have a chance of missing or landing, with ones that land being pretty powerful

summon: summons pets that do cool buffs until they die

each spell has an 8 second cooldown and each pet has a 40 second cooldown


items:
health potion: heals 200 hp, letting you go over your max health of 500

ninja star: does 30 dmg... thats it

strength elixir: triples the damage when you fight or use a ninja star and THEY DONT STACK SO DONT USE MULTIPLE AT ONCE CUZ ITLL STILL ONLY TRIPLE IT!!!!!!!!!!

rusty shield: blocks damage and has a 1 in 3 chance of breaking every turn


spells:

fireball: has a 60% of doing 80 damage

thunderclap: has a 40% chance of doing 140 damage

shadow vortex: has a 25% chance of doing 200 damage and healing 40 hp

ice rain: has a 75% chance of doing 25 damage


summons:
abyss drake: makes your shadow vortex do 800 damage and heal 200 hp for 4 turns, then dies

mini hydra: doubles damage of fight damage for 8 turns, then dies

magic wolf: doubles damage of spell damage for 6 turns, then dies
